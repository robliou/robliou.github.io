Senior full-stack exam project

As part of my interview with a company, Aha-login is a full-stack app I created in order to fulfill their senior full-stack engineer requirement. The requirements were complex, and required a few weeks of my time. They included a React front-end and a Node-backend, and integration with GraphQL as well as Auth0's management API in order to obtain detailed login statistics about not only the user, but all users.
